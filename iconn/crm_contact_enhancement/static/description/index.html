CRM Contact Enhancement

Improves address data entry consistency by reordering fields and requiring Country to be selected before State/City/ZIP is shown.

What’s included

  • Address layout reordered: Country first, then State/City/ZIP, then Street and Street 2.
  • State/City/ZIP are hidden until a Country is selected.
  • Applied to main contact form, child address forms, and private address form.

Where it applies

  • Contacts app (Contact/Customer/Vendor forms)
  • Addresses in the “Contacts & Addresses” tab
  • Private address form
